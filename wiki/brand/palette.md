# Color Palette

**Status:** suggested in the brief, not finalized. No formal decision recorded yet — when colors lock, file a `decisions/NNNN-palette.md` entry.

## Brief-suggested values

| Role | Suggestion | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | Warm teal | `#2D7D7B` or similar — derived from the existing logo (teal/green) but modernized |
| Secondary | Soft sage green | Companion to primary |
| Accent (CTA) | Warm amber / golden | Used for primary CTAs, attention-getting moments |
| Background | Warm off-white | `#FAF8F5` — not pure white, never cold |
| Body text | Dark charcoal | Not pure black |
| Emergency CTA | Warm coral or deep teal | Distinct from accent, but not alarming |

## Rules of thumb

- **Never use default Tailwind blue/indigo** as primary (explicit anti-pattern in [CLAUDE.md](../../CLAUDE.md)).
- **Never pure black on pure white.** Both ends warm slightly.
- Pair warm neutrals with the teal — the warmth is what keeps the palette from reading "healthcare cold."
- Amber/gold accent is sparing — used for primary CTAs, "Coming Soon" badge on Knoxville, key emphasis. Not a body color.
- Emergency bar is "warm but not alarming" — the brief is explicit. Likely warm coral or a deeper variant of primary teal, never a fire-engine red.
