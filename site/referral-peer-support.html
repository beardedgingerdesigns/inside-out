Peer Support Referral

Inside Out Wellness and Advocacy offers Peer Support for Medicaid-insured Iowa residents. Peer support offers guidance and support with both mental health and addiction through lived experience and understanding.

For more information or to speak with one of our Peer Support staff, call 515-642-7070.

Important

This is a voluntary program. Medicaid coverage is required.
