Crisis Stabilization — Adults

For adults 18+ experiencing a mental health crisis

Short-term stay of 3–5 days • Not a hospital setting

Before you refer

Min. 5 days of medication in original labeled bottles
Discharge paperwork from ER/hospital (if applicable)
Client is not currently under the influence of substances
Access Referral Form Woodward • 515-642-4125

Crisis Stabilization — Children

For youth ages 5–17 experiencing a mental health crisis

Short-term stay of 3–5 days • Separate program from adult services

Before you refer

Guardian/parent consent required
Min. 5 days of medication in original labeled bottles
Discharge paperwork from ER/hospital (if applicable)
Access Referral Form Perry • 515-642-7070
