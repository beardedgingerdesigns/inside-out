Who We Are

Change starts from within.

Inside Out Wellness & Advocacy is dedicated to empowering clients with the care and treatment they need for a more fulfilling life, free from stigma and stereotypes.

“

Your Journey, Our Support.

”

With a holistic approach at our core, our services are designed to meet you where you are in your mental health journey. Whether through crisis intervention, transitional living, mobile crisis response, community-based programs, outpatient treatment services, or our specialized co-responder programs, our aim is to equip Iowans with the tools, guidance, and encouragement necessary for achieving successful outcomes.

Our team of qualified professionals is committed to working collaboratively with every individual, ensuring a path forward that is both healing and empowering.

Our Values

These five values guide everything we do — how we treat our clients, our partners, and each other.

Empowerment

We believe in supporting individuals and communities to recognize their own strengths, make informed choices, and take control of their well-being. Empowerment is at the heart of our work — it means meeting people where they are, fostering self-advocacy, and encouraging personal growth without judgment.

Integrity

We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all we do. Integrity guides our decisions and actions, ensuring that we remain accountable to those we serve, our partners, and ourselves. We uphold trust through consistent, respectful, and principled conduct.

Collaboration

We know that lasting change is built through connection and shared purpose. We foster inclusive partnerships — with clients, colleagues, and community stakeholders — rooted in mutual respect and open communication. Collaboration allows us to amplify impact and honor diverse perspectives.

Resilience

We recognize the strength it takes to overcome adversity and remain committed to supporting resilience in every form. Whether at the personal, organizational, or community level, we cultivate environments that encourage healing, adaptability, and the ability to thrive through challenges.

Belonging

We create spaces where everyone feels seen, valued, and safe. Belonging means actively dismantling barriers to inclusion and honoring each person’s identity, experiences, and voice. It is foundational to wellness, advocacy, and equity — and central to how we build community.
