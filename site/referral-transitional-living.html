Transitional Housing Application

Apply for Inside Out's Transitional Living Program — structured, supportive housing with access to therapy, skill-building, peer support, and substance use treatment.

About this program

Individuals will have access to therapy, skill-building, groups, peer support services, and weekly substance use treatment. Employment is a requirement of the program. All individuals will be subject to random drug testing and room searches.
