# No Pricing or Insurance Copy

**Status:** active
**Date:** 2026-05-28
**Scope:** All service pages, sitewide

## Decision

No pricing, insurance, or Medicaid-specific copy on the public site. Remove existing insurance claims (e.g., Mobile Crisis "no insurance needed," "free" language) from all service pages. Route all cost/coverage questions through the crisis line (844-428-3878) or intake conversations.

> **Applied 2026-05-29 (D4).** Insurance/cost copy removed sitewide: crisis pages ("you don't need insurance"), Mobile Crisis ("free," "no cost, no insurance needed"), outpatient ("free evaluation," "insurance figured out"), the Provider Hub "Insurance card" referral items, the "Insurance card" what-to-bring items on Perry/Woodward/Transitional Living, and the "Does insurance cover the stay?" FAQ on Perry/Woodward. The community-support "SSI, Medicaid, housing applications" line is **kept** — it describes a benefits-navigation service, not a pricing claim.

## Rationale

Insurance/cost language on a public site creates liability if inaccurate, changes frequently, and risks turning away people who assume they don't qualify. The org's intake process handles eligibility; the website's job is to get people to call.
