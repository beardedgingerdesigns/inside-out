## TL;DR

Justin needs a website redesign for Inside Out Wellness & Advocacy — an Iowa-based mental health nonprofit. The first deliverable is a single-HTML homepage prototype that establishes the visual design system (typography, color, spacing, components). Subsequent pages build on that foundation. The site serves two distinct audiences (people in crisis + partner providers) and must hit a warm/modern/trustworthy aesthetic without falling into healthcare clichés.

## Key facts established

- **Org:** Inside Out Wellness & Advocacy, insideoutiowa.com.
- **Audiences:** (1) individuals/families in crisis, (2) partner providers (doctors, schools, ERs).
- **Services (8):** Crisis Stabilization Adults, Crisis Stabilization Children (5–17), Transitional Living, Peer Support, Community Support Program, Outpatient Substance Use, Mobile Crisis Response, CIT Program.
- **Locations (3):** Woodward, Perry, Knoxville (Spring 2026 — show "Coming Soon" badge).
- **Crisis line:** 844-428-3878 (persistent emergency bar on every page).
- **Other phones:** Woodward 515-642-4125, Admin 515-642-7070.
- **Email:** info@insideoutiowa.org.
- **Perry address:** 410 12th St., Perry, IA 50220.
- **Top nav order:** Services, Locations, Who We Are, Blog (replaces Resources), Contact Us, Open Positions, Donate (button), Partner Providers (visually distinguished).
- **Color direction:** warm teal primary (`#2D7D7B` suggested), sage secondary, amber/gold accent, warm off-white bg (`#FAF8F5`), charcoal text.
- **Type direction:** humanist serif headings (e.g., DM Serif Display), clean sans body (Inter or DM Sans), 16–18px base.
- **Anti-patterns called out:** healthcare blues, cold whites, generic Tailwind defaults.
