## Conventions for new assets

- Web-optimized formats: `.webp` for photos, `.svg` for logos and icons.
- Keep logos as SVG — never rasterize for general use.
- File names: lowercase, hyphen-separated, descriptive. Drop random hash suffixes when re-saving.
- Photos that aren't yet placed in the design live in [`raw/photos/`](../raw/photos/) until they're picked.
